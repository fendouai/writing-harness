# Cognitive Baseline

Use this when the writing task is trying to change what the reader thinks, does, or feels.

Before drafting, define:

- `K`: what the reader currently misunderstands
- `P`: what process the reader currently uses
- `A`: what emotional stance or attribution pattern the reader currently holds

Then define the target state for each dimension.

## Minimum Baseline Contract

Capture:

- reader
- starting K/P/A
- target K/P/A
- resistance score from 1-10
- attribution direction:
  - external
  - mixed
  - internal

## Questions To Answer

- What boundary or distinction does the reader not yet hold?
- What bad process are they likely to follow today?
- What are they defensive, skeptical, or passive about?
- What should they understand, do, and feel by the end?

If you cannot answer these, the job is not specified enough to engineer.
