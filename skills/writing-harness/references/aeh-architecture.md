# AEH Architecture

Use this when the goal is cognitive change rather than surface cleanup.

## Pattern

- `Assert`
  Index the reader's current belief and lower defensiveness.
- `Exception`
  Break the old frame with a contradiction, data point, or reframing.
- `Handler`
  Install the new model, tool, or decision rule.
- `Log`
  Lock the change in with a memory aid, identity cue, or next action.

## Failure Modes

- No `Assert`: reader feels unseen and resists.
- No `Exception`: the old model never gets displaced.
- No `Handler`: the piece sounds smart but gives no usable path.
- No `Log`: the piece lands but does not stick.

## Rule

Do not let the draft jump into advice before it creates space for that advice.
